Business Communications II

EN102 BUSINESS COMMUNICATIONS II (38 Contact Hours--2 Credits) This course continues the development of the student's ability to produce clear and effective business communication. Assignments support the writing and speaking demands that students will face in a business environment and will focus on proper utilization of word choice, mechanics, and style. (Prerequisite: EN101 Business Communications I)
